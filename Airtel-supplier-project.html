Airtel Nigeria Multi-Market Supplier Performance

Optimization Initiative Dashboard | August 2022 - September 2024

Strategic Challenge

Inconsistent supplier performance across 12 regional markets with limited visibility into product portfolio effectiveness. Required standardized evaluation frameworks to optimize performance for 40M+ customers while ensuring regulatory compliance.

Project Objectives

Develop unified supplier assessment system, optimize product mix for enhanced customer value, ensure 100% regulatory compliance across all markets, and establish predictive analytics for performance optimization.

Implementation Scope

Comprehensive supplier performance management system coordinating with 50+ vendors across 12 regional markets, managing 200+ product offerings with advanced SQL-based tracking and automated KPI reporting.

Comprehensive Vendor Evaluation Matrix

Risk Assessment Protocols
Developed predictive risk models for supplier reliability and market stability assessment. Established early warning systems for potential performance degradation and supply chain disruptions.
Standardized Performance Reviews
Implemented quarterly supplier performance reviews with standardized reporting templates and KPI tracking. Created escalation procedures for underperformance and improvement action plans.
SQL-Based Tracking System
Built comprehensive database infrastructure monitoring 200+ product offerings across 12 regional markets. Implemented real-time data collection and automated performance tracking with historical trend analysis.
Advanced Excel Dashboards
Created executive-level dashboards with automated KPI reporting for management review. Integrated pivot tables, conditional formatting, and dynamic charting for actionable insights.
Predictive Analytics Implementation
Developed predictive models to identify underperforming products and at-risk supplier relationships. Implemented machine learning algorithms for demand forecasting and performance optimization.
Monthly Supplier Review Meetings

Regulatory Body Coordination
