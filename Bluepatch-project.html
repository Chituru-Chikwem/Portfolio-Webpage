Research & Evaluation Framework Development

Designed comprehensive assessment criteria covering sustainability credentials, business viability, and community impact. Created standardized evaluation matrix with weighted scoring for environmental impact, ethical practices, and service quality.

Data Collection & Analysis Implementation

Conducted primary research on 150+ businesses through structured interviews and documentation review. Built Excel-based tracking system with pivot tables and automated reporting for performance metrics.

Stakeholder Coordination & Management

Managed relationships with business owners, community members, and internal teams. Facilitated 50+ stakeholder consultations to gather requirements and feedback from cross-functional teams.
